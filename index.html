-More than 80 levels in a variety of environments, including desert, water, snow, and lava

-Over 50 unique towers for strategic considerations and optimal defense concepts

-No predefined paths; the player directs monsters by building towers strategically

-A rich selection of over 40 enemies that walk, fly and/or swim. Some possess unique abilities, such as druids that transform into animals, or necromancers who summon skeletons

-Endless wave mode and competitive ranking system

-Tutorial and advanced level unlock system to ease beginners into the game

-Unlockable and customizable profile pictures for display on leaderboards

-Environmental factors and enemy strengths/weaknesses that influence gameplay and strategy

-Active post-release support, including bug fixes and potentially free content updates after release
